OUD Sensitivity of Referrals to Wait Time (Dmnl [0,2,0.1])
= GAME (1)
Description: This controls the magnitude of the effect increases in new patient wait time (or decreases in target wait time) have on referrals, where referrals drop as wait time increases (or the target wait time decreases).
